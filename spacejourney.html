Spacejourney (Group)

The goal was concept development for a fictional space program lead by the Danish National Space-program.

What we came up was a fancy space restaurant with a hotel. I was in charge of making the menu site, the logo and a poster.

Since the target group for our case were rich people. We wanted to empathize that they would get an excellent experience that they paid for.

I made the poster trying to put weight on those factors.

While my menu site I had focus on displaying the food in an appetizing way.
